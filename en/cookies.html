Cookie policy

This page explains which information the website stores in your browser.

Necessary storage

The website stores your choice in the browser's local storage under the name subz_cookie_consent_v1. It is only used to remember whether you selected necessary cookies or allowed all.

Analytics and marketing

The website currently has no analytics, statistics or marketing cookies. If this changes, the cookie information and consent solution must be updated.
